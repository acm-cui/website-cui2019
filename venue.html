Venue

CUI 2019 will take place at Science Gallery Dublin in the heart of Dublin city centre.

Getting Here

Getting to Dublin centre
If travelling from the airport attendees can avail of numerous public transport options.
        
Aircoach - Tickets can be purchased online or outside Terminal 1 or 2 (follow the signs for buses). We advise attendees to book tickets in advance where possible when travelling at peak times, particularly for return journeys from the city centre.

The 700 route run by Aircoach will get you to many of the popular stops in the city centre. O'Connell Bridge and Kildare Street stops are a short walk away from Science Gallery Dublin. O'Connell Street and Leeson Street are also close to several popular accommodation spots.

Close to the city centre, return journeys to the airport are popularly accessed from St Stephen's Green, Westmoreland Street and O'Connell Street, amongs others.

Airlink Express also provide numerous stops in the city centre from the airport. 

Getting to Science Gallery Dublin (information provided by Science Gallery Dublin - see more here)

Walking
Science Gallery Dublin is located at the Pearse Street entrance of Trinity College Dublin, opposite The Academy Building, and around the corner from Westland Row and Pearse Street station.

Bus
44, 61 to Pearse St Station or 1, 44, 47, 56a, 61, 77A to Sandwith St. dublinbus.ie

Train
DART and suburban train services stop at Pearse Street Station. irishrail.ie

Taxi
For calling a taxi, the mytaxi app offers reasonable rates.

Parking
The nearest 24hr car park is in Fleet Street, Temple Bar. parkrite.ie

Bike
The closest Dublin Bike station is right outside on Pearse Street dublinbikes.ie

Further information on travelling to and around Dublin can be found at Visit Dublin and Transport for Ireland. For travelling outside of Dublin Fáilte Ireland is a useful resource.
