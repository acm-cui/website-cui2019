Accommodation


Accommodation options can be found through websites like https://www.visitdublin.com/, https://www.booking.com/ & https://www.airbnb.ie. Below are some of the available accommodation options at universities in Dublin,.

Trinity College Summer Accommodation

Trinity College Dublin offer both city centre rooms on the Trinity Campus, as well as suburban rooms in Darty (4km from the city).

Accommodation can be booked through https://www.tcd.ie/summeraccommodation/.

Please note that these rooms are NOT on hold for CUI 2019 and are available to the public too. Trinity College is in the centre of Dublin and these rooms will quickly book out.

Dublin City University Summer Accommodation

Dublin City University offer accommodation in North Dublin. Their All Hallows & St Patrick's accommodation are located in the Drumcondra area, approximately 15 minutes away from the city centre via public transport.

Accommodation can be booked through https://www.dcurooms.com.

As above, these rooms are NOT on hold for CUI 2019 and are available to the public too. Trinity College is in the centre of Dublin and these rooms will quickly book out.
